On the Easel

Art imitates life

Art has always been such a presence in my life. From a young age, I was drawn to nature – the beauty of plants, animals, insects, really everything that this earth has to offer. I started capturing all the beauty that I saw by drawing and painting. Sitting hours outside at a time, surrounding myself in nature.

After continuing to focus on creating art throughout my childhood and teenage years, I was then accepted into the Art Center College of Design as an adult to continue my studies and attain a BFA and MFA. While I have shown at a gallery level, I truly enjoy working in my own studio, setting up appointments and inviting buyers and shoppers to come visit me in my space.
